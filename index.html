WHAT IS U KARKA?

U Kar Ka is a Myanmar export company which is introducing the healthiest and most hygienic Laphat and Myanmar Tea to the World. It been an established traditional organic Myanmar tea company since 1928. Our business model is based on meeting the needs of the Myanmar people, and providing the best quality Myanmar Tea and Laphat at the most affordable prices. To do this we offer different types of dried and pickled tea, and use a combination of wholesale and retail marketing strategies to get our products to the people.

OUR PRODUCTS

U Kar Ka, a Myanmar Export company, offers a range of exceptional products that customers can enjoy for all occasions, at home, and on the go

To provide some examples, Zayan Chin Tea, one of our best-selling Myanmar Tea, was processed in the Zayan region with age-old techniques. Its tea leaves a tangy refreshing taste on your tongue followed by a sweet aroma that lingers in your mouth after consumption. We also have our own Jasmine Tea, which is carefully selected and blended to perfection. Other popular products include Hlaw Thin, a premium Burmese tea that gives off a sweet roasted aroma.

BEING A RESPONSIBLE EXPORT COMPANY

Here at U Kar Ka, we believe in the importance of building a great, enduring Myanmar export company that strikes a balance between perpetual growth and social conscience. We respect the potential that Myanmar green tea or laphet has and how it can bring growth for the farmers and tea growers in our country.

ETHICAL SOURCING

We make sure that ethics and quality comes first. We follow responsible purchasing procedures while supporting local farmers. We make sure that the growth of our company also means the growth of their businesses. Throughout the life of our company we have built meaningful relationships with our suppliers which create a long-term supply of high-quality tea leaves that we have been blending, roasting, and packing them fresh since 1928.

ENVIRONMENTAL AWARENESS

As a company that is working with naturally produced products, we believe in the importance of nature and the environment. We believe in the importance of caring for our planet and encouraging others to do the same, while we ourselves are working to reduce our environmental footprint through energy and water conservation, recycling and thinking green.

GIVING BACK TO THE COMMUNITY

Donations and community awareness is a big part of our company culture. Ranging from small tea farms in northern Myanmar to the local neighborhood stores, we at U Kar Ka believes in giving back and fostering thriving communities.
